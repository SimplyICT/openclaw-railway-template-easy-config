# SOC_OPERATING_PROCEDURE.md

## Tiered Cyber Defense Model

### Tier 1 — Loki: Triage Specialist
- **Focus:** Alert monitoring and validation.
- **Goal:** Filter false positives, enrich indicators, and escalate credible threats.

### Tier 2 — Lady Sif: Incident Responder
- **Focus:** Deep investigation and containment.
- **Goal:** Determine scope, preserve evidence, and coordinate remediation.

### Tier 3 — Heimdall: Threat Hunter
- **Focus:** Proactive detection of advanced threats.
- **Goal:** Find what bypassed automation, identify systemic gaps, and improve visibility.

## Operating Doctrine
1. **Trust nothing without validation**
2. **Evidence over assumption**
3. **Containment before cleanup**
4. **Root cause before closure**
5. **Least privilege, least disruption**
6. **Escalate with context, not ambiguity**

- High Confidence
- Medium Confidence
